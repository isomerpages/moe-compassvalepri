#### **CVPS Open House 2026 Information**


|  | Webinar via Microsoft Teams | Physical School Tour |
| -------- | -------- | -------- |
| Date     | Saturday, 11 July 2026     | Friday, 17 July 2026     |
| Time     | 10a.m. to 11a.m.     | 2.15p.m. to 4.30p.m.     |
| Registration deadline     | 6 July     | 13 July     | 

**<u>The Webinar via Microsoft Teams</u>** is designed to provide parents with a comprehensive introduction to our school through a convenient and accessible online platform. The session will highlight key aspects of our educational experience, including our curriculum, student care services, Mother Tongue Language (MTL) programmes, Co-Curricular Activities (CCAs), NASCANS, and the Primary 1 journey. To further support parents in gaining a well-rounded understanding of our school, additional information and resources will also be available on our school website.

**<u>The Physical School Tour</u>** is designed to provide parents and prospective students with an authentic glimpse into our vibrant school environment and culture. Through guided tours of the school grounds and dynamic CCA showcases, families will have the opportunity to observe our students in action and gain first-hand insight into our holistic educational approach, learning facilities, and school spirit. Please note that the programme does not include classroom visits, lesson observations, or classroom-based learning experiences. In addition, food and beverages will not be available for purchase from the school canteen during the tour. 

**Registration for both the Webinar and/or Physical School Tour will open on 27 June 2026.**

* Webinar login details will be emailed to registered participants three days before the event.
* Guided tour slots will be confirmed, and a confirmation email will be sent to registrants at least three days before the tour date.


#### **Open House Registration Guidelines**

Please note that only parents or legal guardians are eligible to register their child(ren) for our Open House. Kindergartens or schools are not permitted to register on behalf of students or bring groups of students to the event.

We kindly ask that all registrants adhere to these guidelines when signing up.

Should you have any questions, please feel free to contact us at +65 6388 2819.

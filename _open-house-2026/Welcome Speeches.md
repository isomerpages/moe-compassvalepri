<p>Dear Prospective P1 Parents,</p>
<p>As you walk through our halls today, you are witnessing more than classrooms
and corridors – you are seeing the nurturing ground where your child will
take their very first steps into primary school life and where extraordinary
potential will flourish. This is where their love for learning begins,
where friendships are formed and where they grow into confident, curious
young learners.</p>
<p>At Compassvale Primary, we believe every student is a Thinker of Tomorrow,
equipped with curiosity and critical thinking skills that will serve them
in an ever-evolving world.</p>
<p>Beyond academic excellence, we are committed to developing Leaders of
Character – gaining respect by leading with responsibility, integrity and
care. Our students learn that true leadership is not about titles or positions,
but about the positive impact they make on others.</p>
<p>Most importantly, we nurture Contributors of Community who understand
that their individual success is intertwined with the wellbeing of others.
From voluntary community service projects to global awareness initiatives,
our students discover the joy and responsibility of making a difference.</p>
<p>Thank you for taking time to learn more about Compassvale Primary School.
We look forward to partnering with you in building a strong foundation
for your child – one who will think deeply, lead with purpose, and contribute
meaningfully to our world.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Warm Regards,</p>
<p>Mr Daniel Tham</p>
<p>Chairman, School Advisory Committee</p>

<h4><strong>Chairperson, Parent Support Group</strong></h4>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 50%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/sac4.jpg">

<p>Dear Prospective P1 Parents,</p>
<p>Welcome to Compassvale Primary School (CVPS)! Thank you for taking the
time to explore our school through this Open House. Choosing a school is
a significant step, and we hope that hearing from our teachers and parents
of Compassvalites will give you a clearer sense of what the journey here
feels like.</p>
<p>At CVPS, we are committed to creating a happy and safe learning environment
where children truly enjoy learning, and where they feel comfortable with
their teachers and classmates. That sense of belongings matters deeply
to us, as we believe that when children feel at ease, they are free to
discover their joy of learning and grow into curious, confident and happy
learners.</p>
<p>Children love it when their parents get to see where they learn and play.
We warmly encourage you to join our Parent Support Group (PSG), volunteer
when you can, and journey alongside your child through these formative
years. Being part of the PSG brings reassurance, friendship, and shared
encouragement, helping you feel more connected to your child’s school life
from the very beginning.</p>
<p>As we like to say, “A child grows best when home and school walk hand
in hand.” Our PSG is built on the values of a strong home-school partnerships,
and we are here to help bridge parents and the school through open communication,
meaningful collaboration and mutual support. We are truly grateful for
the strong parent community we have at CVPS – your partnership and presence
make all the difference.</p>
<p>We look forward to welcoming you at our Open House and helping you discover
CVPS with confidence and heart.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Warm regards,&nbsp;
<br>Celina Teo&nbsp;
<br>Chairperson, Parent Support Group&nbsp;</p>

### **Basketball**

<center><img src="/images/Year%202025/2025%20CCA/Basketball_4_R.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **What We Do**
Basketball develops agility, teamwork and game intelligence. Through structured training sessions, students learn fundamental skills, offensive/defensive strategies and competitive gameplay under the guidance of qualified coaches.<br>

##### **What Students Will Learn**
* **Fundamental basketball skills** such as ball handling, shooting, passing and coordinated footwork
* **Game understanding**, including basic offensive and defensive strategies, spacing and court awareness
* **Physical development**, building stamina, agility, speed and overall fitness through progressive training
* **Character-building values** such as teamwork, resilience, discipline and sportsmanship through practice and gameplay<br>
<center><img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/Basketball_1.jpg" style="width:65%">
</center>
##### **Opportunities for Students**
* School representation in National School Games competitions
* Friendly matches and internal games
* Mentorship opportunities where seniors guide juniors during training and team activities.
<center><img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/Basketball_2R.jpg" style="width:65%">
</center>
##### **Who Should Join**
* Students with energy and enthusiasm for fast-paced sports
* Students who enjoy teamwork and competitive play

### **Football**
<center>
<img src="/images/Year%202025/2025%20CCA/Football_3_R.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **What We Do**<br>
Football builds teamwork, coordination and tactical thinking. Players undergo regular training to strengthen technical skills like dribbling, passing and shooting, while learning field strategy and positional play.<br><br>
<img src="/images/Year%202025/2025%20CCA/Football_1_R.jpg" style="width:49%" align="left">
<img src="/images/Year%202025/2025%20CCA/Football_2_R.jpg" style="width:49%" align="right">
<br clear="left">
<br clear="right">

##### **What Students Will Learn**<br>
* Ball control, tackling and shooting techniques
* Field positioning and movement off the ball
* Endurance, agility and strength
* Communication and cooperation on the pitch

##### **Opportunities for Students**<br>
* National School Games competitions
* School tournaments and friendlies 
* Guide and mentor juniors during training sessions

* Students who enjoy outdoor sports and fast-paced games
* Students passionate about working together as a team

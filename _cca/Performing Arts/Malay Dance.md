### **Malay Dance**

<center><img src="/images/Year%202025/2025%20CCA/Malay_Dance_2_R.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **What We Do**<br>
Malay Dance introduces students to traditional and contemporary Malay dance forms that celebrate grace, fluidity and cultural identity. Members train in foundational dance movements, musical interpretation and polished stage presentation, learning to move confidently to a variety of Malay music styles.<br><br>

##### **What Students Will Learn**<br>
* Fundamental Malay dance steps and gestures
* Graceful body control, poise and coordination
* Musical expression and timing
* Ensemble synchronization and teamwork
<center><img src="/images/Malaydance3.jpeg" style="width:65%">
</center>

##### **Opportunities for Students**<br>
* School celebrations and cultural events
* Community showcases
* Competitions and Singapore Youth Festival Arts Presentation
<center><img src="/images/Year%202025/2025%20CCA/Malay_Dance_1_R.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **Who Should Join**<br>
* Students who enjoy graceful movement and performing for audiences
* Students who appreciate Malay cultural arts and expression

### **Harmonica Ensemble**
In Harmonica, students will be exposed to a range of harmonicas and other musical instruments. The students will also acquire and develop school core values as they train and perform together.

Members will be trained to play the chromatic harmonica.Â  Those who have good grasp in the fundamentals of music such as good rhythm sense will be able to progress faster to more advanced instruments. Members in the Harmonica Ensemble will also have the opportunities to be trained to play the chromatic harmonica, bass harmonica, chord harmonica, marimba, double bass or piano.

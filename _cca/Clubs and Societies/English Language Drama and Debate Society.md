### **ELDDS (English Language Drama and Debate Society)**

<center><img src="/images/Year%202025/2025%20CCA/ELDDS_2_R.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **What We Do**<br>
ELDDS builds confident communicators through debate, drama, public speaking and performance. Members develop creativity, expressive skills and critical thinking as they explore scripts, improvise scenes and work in ensembles. <br>
<center><img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/ELDDS_1.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **What Students Will Learn**<br>
* Drama techniques and stagecraft
* Speech clarity, projection and confidence
* Creative storytelling
* Collaboration in group rehearsals
* Critical thinking through discussions and reflection <br>
<center><img src="/images/english2.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **Opportunities for Students**<br>
* School performances and assemblies
* Competitions and public speaking platforms <br>
<center><img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/ELDDS_2.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **Who Should Join**<br>
* Students who enjoy acting, speaking or performing
* Students who want to build confidence and communication skills <br>

##### **What We Do**<br>
Science Club brings curiosity to life through hands-on experiments and real-world investigations. Members explore scientific concepts in fun, engaging ways while building problem-solving and inquiry skills. <br><br>
<img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/Physical_Science_5.jpg" style="width:33%" align="left">
<img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/Physical_Science_7.jpg" style="width:33%" align="left"> 
<img src="/images/Year%202025/2025%20CCA/Physical_Science_3_R.jpg" style="width:33%" align="left"> 
<br clear="left">

##### **What Students Will Learn**<br>
* Scientific method and experimentation
* Chemistry, physics and environmental science basics
* Teamwork through group investigations
* Creativity in designing experiments and solutions <br>
<center><img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/Physical_Science_12R.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **Opportunities for Students**<br>
* School-based science fairs
* Participation in external science challenges <br>
<img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/Physical_Science_15.jpg" style="width:49%" align="left"><img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/Physical_Science_10.jpg" style="width:49%" align="right">
<br clear="left">
<br clear="right">

##### **Who Should Join**<br>
* Students curious about how things work
* Students who enjoy hands-on learning and discovery <br>

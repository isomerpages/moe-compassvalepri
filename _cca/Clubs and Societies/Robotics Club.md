##### **What We Do**<br>
Robotics combines creativity, coding and engineering. Members learn to build and program robots to solve challenges and complete missions using STEM principles.
<center><img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/Robotics_1.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **What Students Will Learn**<br>
* Robot construction and design
* Coding and problem-solving
* Logical thinking and teamwork
* Iteration and innovation <br>
<center><img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/Robotics_3.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **Opportunities for Students**<br>
* Robotics competitions and showcases
* Inter-school STEM events <br>
<center><img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/Robotics_5R.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **Who Should Join**<br>
* Students interested in technology, engineering or programming
* Students who enjoy hands-on building and logical challenges <br>

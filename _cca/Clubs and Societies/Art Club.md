### **Art Club**
<center><img src="/images/Year%202025/2025%20CCA/Art_Club_2_R.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **What We Do**<br>
Art Club provides a creative space for students to explore visual arts through hands-on activities. Members experiment with different mediums, techniques and themes while expressing ideas through art.<br>
<center><img src="/images/Year%202025/2025%20CCA/Art_Club_1_R.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **What Students Will Learn**<br>
* Drawing, painting, sculpting and mixed-media skills
* Creativity, visual communication and design thinking
* Art appreciation and critique skills
* Patience, craftsmanship and attention to detail <br>
<center><img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/Art_Club_2.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **Opportunities for Students**<br>
* School exhibitions and art showcases
* Singapore Youth Festival Art Presentation and community art competitions <br>
<center><img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/Art_Club_1R.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **Who Should Join**<br>
* Students who enjoy creating, exploring and expressing through art
* Students eager to try new techniques and materials <br>

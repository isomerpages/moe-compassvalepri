### **Red Cross Youth**
<center><img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/Red_Cross_2.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **What We Do**<br>
Red Cross Youth equips students with essential life skills while instilling compassion, leadership and a spirit of service. Members learn first aid, emergency preparedness and community service values. <br>
<center><img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/Red_Cross_1.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **What Students Will Learn**<br>
* First aid knowledge and safety awareness
* Emergency response and preparedness
* Leadership and teamwork
* Responsibility, resilience and service values <br>
<center><img src="/images/2024%20Open%20House/CCA/Red_Cross_3.jpg" style="width:65%"></center>

##### **Opportunities for Students** <br>
* School and community service projects
* Red Cross Youth events and camps

##### **Who Should Join** <br>
* Students who want to help others and contribute to the community
* Students who enjoy hands-on learning and teamwork <br>
